### Example Sentence

Complete the sentence:

The algorithm sometimes _______ consumer behavior.

<details>
  <summary>Show answer</summary>

**Answer:**  
The algorithm sometimes **distorts** consumer behavior.

**Note:**  
“distort” means to change something so that it is not accurate or fair.
